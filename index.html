Professional Profile

Ben Scott, Ad. E., is a preeminent cross-border litigator and strategic advisor with over two decades of experience at the highest levels of the profession. As a Partner and Consultant at BRL & Associates—a law firm with offices in both Montréal and New York—he is routinely retained by multinational corporations, financial institutions, sovereign entities, and high-net-worth individuals, including celebrities and public figures, to handle their most sensitive and high-stakes disputes.

Called to the bars of Québec, Ontario, and New York, Mr. Scott has served as lead counsel in landmark trials, appeals, and international arbitrations across North America and Europe. His practice sits at the intersection of complex commercial litigation, white-collar defence, and cross-border regulatory enforcement—areas in which he is consistently ranked among the top tier of practitioners worldwide.

He holds the prestigious Advocatus Emeritus (Ad. E.) distinction from the Barreau du Québec, an honour awarded to select members who have demonstrated a career of exceptional professional excellence and contribution to the legal community—comparable to the title of King's Counsel (KC) in common-law jurisdictions. He is also a Fellow of both the American College of Trial Lawyers and the International Academy of Trial Lawyers, the two most selective invitation-only trial advocacy organizations in North America.

Mr. Scott has been recognized in Chambers Canada (Band 1, Litigation: General Commercial – Québec), Benchmark Litigation's Top 50 Trial Lawyers in Canada, the Lexpert® Leading 500 Cross-Border Lawyers, The Best Lawyers in Canada, and Who's Who Legal: International Arbitration. He is a Past President of the Canadian Bar Association, Québec Division.

A graduate of McGill University and Osgoode Hall Law School, Mr. Scott is fluent in English, French, and Spanish, and is known for his courtroom tenacity, strategic vision, and absolute discretion.
